About Us

Harpal Singh Sodhi

Born in a village in 1969, Harpal Singh Came to Delhi in 1974 in search of work. After early demise of his father he have all the reponsibility of feeding his family of 20 people. At a very young age of 12, he sold out his ancestors old fields and bought this shop. For first five years the shop was in deep loss, he almost decided to close the shop and do something else but his mom forced him no to do so and give it a one last try. So after deep thinking he decided not to do so and learn ways to find how the top competitors in market works. After months of Visualisation he found out that most important in field is attrackting customer. So he started learning new tectics to attract customer. Then on 14 Nov. 1985, he announced a massive sale with huge discounts. A total of worht ₹ 15000 suits sold out that day, although there was no net profit that day due to huge sales but he got many new customers. After that day the buisness keeps growing till date. Due to our focus on quality most of the new customers become our regular customers. Nowadays we sell daily goods worth ₹ 5 Lakhs.
We contritube our 10% net profit to Feed India N.G.O. Always Our ultimate goal have been to give best possible service and product to our customer not only pre sales but even after sales you can call us and consult us.
